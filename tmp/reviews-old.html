Outdoor Space Brought To Life

Working with S. Phaneuf & Associates was an absolute pleasure from start to finish. We had a vision for our outdoor space, and they brought it to life beyond our expectations. The custom fabricated fire pit and gas torches in Stonewall are the centerpiece of our backyard, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere for gatherings. The granite stairs leading to our plunge pool add a touch of elegance, while the outdoor kitchen with blue stone countertops makes entertaining a breeze. We also appreciate the attention to detail shown in the separate wood-burning fire pit and the thin veneer fieldstone on our basement walls, which seamlessly blend with the overall design. Throughout the entire process, the team at S. Phaneuf & Associates demonstrated professionalism, expertise, and a commitment to delivering high-quality results. We couldn't be happier with the outcome and would highly recommend them to anyone looking to elevate their outdoor living space. Anonymous - Concord, MA

Attention To Detail

In addition, they repaired and stuccoed (refinished with a smooth concrete surface) our existing back stairs. All three tasks were performed with an admirable attention to detail, and for a very decent price. Beyond that, the guys working at my house were really friendly, and polite. Can I just mention, that the stucco job they did on my stairs is really pretty? Not just a job well done, but pretty. The concrete is so finely worked that it resembles fondant icing, it's THAT smooth. That's good work, right there. They do much more than what they did for me. All kinds of hardscapes and stonework. Look to their website. Would I hire them, again? You betcha! Kerri O. - Maynard, MA
