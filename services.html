Masonry & Hardscapes

Stone, slate, brick. These materials can significantly enhance the beauty of your property, add to its value, and provide years of enjoyment in the form of stone walkways, paver stone patios, stone walls and steps, stone veneer, outdoor kitchens and fire pits, and more! New England's climate can be rough on stonework or masonry. But not to worry…our experienced approach combines quality materials and smart design to construct strong, long lasting masonry and stonework for outdoor living spaces that add natural beauty and value to your home or business.

Hardscapes, as it is referred to in the Landscape Industry, is one of the most natural ways to add beauty to your landscape. Landscape construction of hardscapes can define outdoor space in the same way that plantings beautifies outdoor space. It can help landscape become architecture.
