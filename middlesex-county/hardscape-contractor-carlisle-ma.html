Materials We Work With

Stone

Stone offers timeless beauty and durability, perfect for patios, walkways, and garden features that require minimal upkeep.

Brick

Brick adds an elegant, traditional touch to any hardscape project. It’s perfect for patios, walkways, and retaining walls, providing long-lasting beauty and strength.

Pavers

Pavers offer endless possibilities for design. Available in many shapes, colors, and sizes, they are ideal for creating durable, custom patios and walkways.

Gravel

Gravel is an affordable, versatile option for paths, drainage solutions, and garden ground cover. It adds a natural, rustic feel to any landscape.

Concrete

Concrete is a strong and versatile material for a range of outdoor spaces. Whether smooth or textured, it’s ideal for patios, driveways, and walkways.

Tile

Tile is a beautiful and moisture-resistant material, ideal for poolside areas and outdoor kitchens, offering durability and style in many finishes.

Wood

Wood adds warmth and character to any outdoor structure. Perfect for decks, pergolas, and arbors, wood creates an inviting, natural atmosphere.

Composite Decking

Composite decking combines the aesthetic beauty of wood with the durability of synthetic materials, making it a low-maintenance and eco-friendly option for decks and pool areas.
