Materials We Work With

Stone

Stone is a classic material for patios, walkways, and garden features, offering both durability and timeless beauty for your outdoor space.

Brick

Brick is perfect for creating elegant and long-lasting features like patios, paths, and retaining walls. Its warm, classic look adds a sophisticated touch to your landscape.

Pavers

Pavers offer a range of design possibilities with different colors, shapes, and patterns. They’re ideal for creating customized patios, walkways, and driveways that stand out and endure for years.

Gravel

Gravel is an affordable and versatile material ideal for paths, drainage solutions, and natural landscaping. It provides a rustic charm to your Wayland yard while being eco-friendly and low-maintenance.

Concrete

Concrete is highly durable and adaptable, perfect for creating a range of outdoor surfaces, including patios, walkways, and driveways. Its low-maintenance nature makes it a practical choice for high-traffic areas.

Tile

Tile offers a sleek, water-resistant solution for pool decks, outdoor kitchens, and other areas exposed to moisture. It’s available in a variety of styles and colors to fit your design needs.

Wood

Wood adds warmth and natural beauty to your outdoor areas. Whether for decks, pergolas, or other structures, it creates an inviting and rustic atmosphere in your Wayland landscape.

Composite Decking

Composite decking combines the beauty of wood with the durability and low-maintenance qualities of synthetic materials, offering a long-lasting, eco-friendly option for your outdoor spaces.
