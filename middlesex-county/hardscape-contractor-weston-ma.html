Materials We Work With

Stone

Stone is a durable and elegant choice for patios, walkways, and garden features. It provides a timeless look while requiring little maintenance, making it perfect for long-lasting outdoor spaces.

Brick

Brick adds sophistication and charm to your hardscape projects. Whether used for walkways, patios, or retaining walls, brick offers a classic aesthetic with excellent durability.

Pavers

Pavers are a versatile option for creating custom patios, walkways, and driveways. Available in various colors and patterns, pavers offer endless design possibilities and are built to last.

Gravel

Gravel is a flexible and affordable material perfect for creating paths, drainage solutions, or garden beds. It adds a natural touch to your landscape while allowing for easy water flow.

Concrete

Concrete is an ideal material for creating smooth, durable surfaces like patios, driveways, and walkways. It’s a practical, low-maintenance option for high-traffic areas.

Tile

Tile is a popular choice for poolside areas and outdoor kitchens. It’s available in a variety of finishes and provides a durable, slip-resistant surface that’s perfect for outdoor use in Weston.

Wood

Wood brings warmth and natural beauty to outdoor spaces. Perfect for decks, pergolas, and other structures, it creates an inviting atmosphere in your Weston yard.

Composite Decking

Composite decking combines the look of wood with the durability of synthetic materials, making it an eco-friendly, low-maintenance choice for decks and pool areas.
