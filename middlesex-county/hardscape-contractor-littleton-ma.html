Materials We Work With

Stone

Stone is a classic choice for patios, pathways, and garden features, offering both beauty and durability for low-maintenance outdoor spaces.

Brick

Brick brings sophistication and elegance to any hardscape project. It's ideal for walkways, patios, and retaining walls, combining durability with a timeless look.

Pavers

Pavers provide endless design possibilities, available in various colors and patterns. They're perfect for creating custom patios, walkways, and driveways that are both durable and visually appealing.

Gravel

Gravel is an affordable, versatile option ideal for creating paths, drainage solutions, and natural ground covers in your garden.

Concrete

Concrete offers both strength and versatility. Whether you're creating smooth or textured surfaces, concrete is an excellent option for patios, driveways, and walkways.

Tile

Tile is an excellent choice for poolside areas and outdoor kitchens, offering a variety of finishes and styles that are both moisture-resistant and durable.

Wood

Wood brings warmth to outdoor spaces. Perfect for decks, pergolas, and other structures, it creates an inviting, natural atmosphere.

Composite Decking

Composite decking combines the beauty of wood with the durability of synthetic materials. It's a low-maintenance, eco-friendly choice for decks and pool areas.
