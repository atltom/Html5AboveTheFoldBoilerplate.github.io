Our Company

S Phanuef & Associates was established with a vision to provide residents and businesses in the New England area with reliable, professional asphalt paving, excavation, masonry, and hardscape solutions. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to integrity, Steve Phaneuf set out to build a company that would exceed expectations in every project undertaken.

Our mission is simple: to transform your vision into reality. Whether you're looking to enhance your property with stunning masonry work or improve its functionality with expert paving services, we're here to deliver outstanding results that stand the test of time.

We are separated from other companies in New England by our high quality service and affordable costs. Your project doesn’t have to come with a premium price-tag. In fact, you can get the professional results you deserve without investing massive amounts of income. Our local crew has the materials, skills, and experience to deliver a powerful experience that you’ll be happy with.
